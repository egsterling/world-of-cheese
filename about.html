About Us

Our Origin

World of Cheese was co-founded by expert cheesemakers LeBron James and Danny Devito all the way back in the year 1902.
The two of them had a vision for the future: they wanted to change the way that people saw cheese in a fundamental way.  As soon as they began working together, they slowly began expanding the company and improving their craft. Now, over 100 years later, their hard work has culminated into one of the largest cheese manufacturers in the United States, one which is still growing today. Although our founders are long retired and have moved onto other endeavours, they will always have a lasting impact in the cheesemaking industry.
